## Attribution Legend

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ | **Direct output** — Paper explicitly acknowledges this grant |
| 🔶 | **Related output** — Thematically relevant but no direct acknowledgment |
| ❌ | **Pre-grant** — Published before grant period |
| — | Not applicable |

## Notes

- Only papers with ✅ may be listed as 「直接的成果」 in F-7/F-19 forms
- Papers with 🔶 may be listed as 「関連成果」 with appropriate labeling
- Papers with ❌ must NOT appear in any grant report
- When in doubt, check the paper's acknowledgment section (PDF level)
